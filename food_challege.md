# 🍜 Simulation Seed: Viral Food Trend Explosion

## Scenario Title

The Rise of “Fire Noodles Ice Cream”

---

## Background

A random food creator posts a bizarre dish called **“Fire Noodles Ice Cream”** — a mix of spicy ramen and vanilla ice cream.

Within 24 hours:

* The video hits 10 million views
* Influencers start reacting
* Some love it, others hate it

The trend begins spreading across platforms.

---

## Key Entities (Agents)

### 🎥 Content Creators

* Objective: Gain views, followers, virality
* Behavior: Trend-chasing, experimental, attention-driven

### 👥 General Users

* Objective: Entertainment, social validation
* Behavior: Easily influenced, reactive, emotional

### 🍔 Food Brands

* Objective: Profit from trends
* Behavior: Opportunistic, fast-moving

### 📱 Social Media Algorithms

* Objective: Maximize engagement
* Behavior: Amplifies controversial/high-engagement content

### 📰 Media

* Objective: Capture attention
* Behavior: Sensationalizes trends

---

## Initial Conditions

* Trend is unknown
* One viral video exists
* Public sentiment is mixed curiosity

---

## Trigger Event

The original video crosses **10M views in 24 hours**

---

## Key Variables

* Virality score (low ↔ explosive)
* Public sentiment (love ↔ hate)
* Participation rate (few ↔ mass adoption)
* Brand involvement (none ↔ global rollout)
* Trend lifespan (short ↔ long-lasting)

---

## Possible Interactions

* Influencers recreate or mock the trend
* Users try it and post reactions
* Brands launch official versions
* Media declares it “the next big thing” or “worst trend ever”
* Algorithm boosts controversy

---

## Constraints

* Attention span of users is short
* New trends compete constantly
* Negative reactions can boost visibility

---

## Simulation Goals

* Predict whether the trend goes global or dies quickly
* Identify peak virality moment
* Analyze role of controversy in growth
* Determine if brands successfully monetize it

---

## Time Horizon

Short-term: 1 week
Mid-term: 1 month
Long-term: 3 months

---

## Notes

This scenario tests viral dynamics, social influence, and emergent behavior in a digital ecosystem.
